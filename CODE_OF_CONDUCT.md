# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Be respectful, constructive, and specific. Welcome users with different levels
of experience, assume good faith, and criticize ideas or reproducible behavior
rather than people. Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention,
doxxing, and publication of another person's private prompts, paths, media, or
credentials are not acceptable.

## Project spaces

These standards apply to repository issues, pull requests, discussions,
security reports, and other spaces where someone is representing this project.
Maintainers may edit, hide, reject, or remove contributions that violate these
standards and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation.

## Reporting

Report conduct involving sensitive or private information through GitHub's
[private vulnerability reporting](https://github.com/ALX-CODE/lingbot-video-1.3b-fp8/security/advisories/new)
without including unrelated secrets or personal data. For an ordinary public
moderation problem, use the report controls on the relevant issue or comment.

Enforcement decisions will consider context, impact, repetition, and safety.
Maintainers will disclose only the information necessary to resolve a report.
